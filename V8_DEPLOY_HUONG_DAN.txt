LUU TRU MEDIA
A. Che do production de xuat:
- Tao mot upload gateway cho Cloudflare R2/S3.
- Dat bien moi truong:
  MEDIA_UPLOAD_ENDPOINT=https://.../upload
  MEDIA_UPLOAD_TOKEN=...
- Gateway nhan binary body, header x-file-key va tra JSON {"url":"https://..."}.

B. Neu chua cau hinh kho media:
- He thong luu vao public/uploads de demo/local.
- Tren Render filesystem co the mat khi redeploy, khong dung cho du lieu production lau dai.
